Sean Kim is a Korean-style chef and baker. He worked at Seoul's Grand Hyatt Hotel for more than 10 years. His dishes have been eaten by celebrities, such as Tom Cruise, Barack Obama and Queen Elizabeth II. In 2015, Kim and his wife Jiyoo moved to Canada and settled in Kitchener-Waterloo. After managing Camridge's beloved DiPietro Bakery, Kim decided to open his own restauraunt. Our community is home to many kitchens, but one thing is certain about Kim's Kitchen—chicken will never be the same.
